<objective>
Surface the discovered source range and the rolling pages/min, candidates/min, and ETA metrics in the
`RunSummary` (RANGE-05), and wire the two Wave-1 config knobs into `runOnce` through the cli. Metrics
derive from the injected-clock page timestamps captured in Plan 04; ETA is present only when a parsed
last-page upper bound is known and is explicitly labelled an estimate, otherwise absent.

Purpose: An operator reads pacing health and remaining-time at the top of the summary instead of guessing.
The full greppable per-page progress-event taxonomy stays deferred to Phase 11 (out of scope here).
Output: extended `RunSummary` type + additive-spread derivation, the cli wiring, and extended colocated tests.
</objective>
