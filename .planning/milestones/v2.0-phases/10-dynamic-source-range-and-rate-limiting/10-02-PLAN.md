<objective>
Create the reusable pacing-floor seam that replaces `createPacedSourceClient`'s blanket 2000ms delay
(RANGE-04). A single `requestSpacingMs` knob is applied as the floor between sequential list pages and,
later, as the minimum spacing inside the concurrency limiter — computed from an injected clock so it
never double-counts the per-request backoff that lives in `withRetry`.

Purpose: Concurrency does the throughput; pacing stays polite without stacking on top of jittered
backoff (Pitfall 2). A pure, injectable seam keeps the ~1–2h target deterministic and testable.
Output: `src/source/pacing.ts` (`createPacer`) + colocated `pacing.test.ts` at 100% V8 coverage.
</objective>
