<objective>
Add a distinct `skippedBySourceId` counter to the run-summary contract so the watch pre-fetch skip
(Plan 03) is observable and auditable [src: 24-CONTEXT.md#DEDUP-01]. Because counts are derived in
`countRun` from the staging/rawStorage arrays and a pre-fetch skip produces NEITHER, the count is
threaded into `buildRunSummary` as an optional input that defaults to 0 — keeping the run-once
stdout contract byte-identical while letting the watch loop supply the real skip total
[src: 24-RESEARCH.md#Pitfall-4]. Implements the counter half of DEDUP-01 and the
distinct-counter requirement of DEDUP-02.

Purpose: a no-new-replay watch cycle must report its skip via its OWN bucket, never folded into
`skipped` (raw not_stageable) or `duplicate` (staging already_staged) [src: 24-RESEARCH.md#Pitfall-5].
Output: an updated `RunSummaryCounts` type, `emptyCounts`, `countRun`, and `buildRunSummary` plus
summary unit tests asserting the new field everywhere it must appear.
</objective>
